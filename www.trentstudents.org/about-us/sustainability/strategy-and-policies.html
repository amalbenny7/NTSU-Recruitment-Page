NTSU is currently in the implementation phase of our new sustainability strategy.

We are proud to release our first ever Sustainability Strategy for NTSU.

Within this ambitious strategy, we commit to improve our environmental sustainability through seven key areas of activity. 

Read the full strategy to learn about our sustainability journey, and the actions we are taking moving forwards.
